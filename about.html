Problems we are solving

In South Africa, more than 86% of small-scale rely heavily on informal & municipal markets to sell their produce.

The Problems with these markets are:

  • They are offline
  • Creating a lot of Waste
  • No visibility on demand/supply
  • Fluctuating Prices & High logistics costs
  • At an inconvenience to the Informal Traders who must physically go to the markets to buy stock, stand in long queues and pay for delivery back to their market stall, loosing up to 35% of selling time.

Farmer

  • Invisible demand.
  • Unstable Pricing.
  • Long payment cycle.
  • Income loss & Produce waste.

Trader

  • Invisible supply.
  • Low individual buying power.
  • Inconvinience & High Delivery cost.

Our Solution

  • Geo-Trading A trader mapping feature enabling traders in one location to Bulk-buy in groups and farmers to share logistics
  • Whatsapp Chatbot A Chat-bot that can collect orders from traders & match to available inventory from farmers in multi-language.
  • Buy-Now-Pay-Later A feature that allows Traders to order stock & pay later, based on alternative credit scores, to improve their buying power
  • Free Delivery in 24 hours Available for all traders ordering through our platform, turning a luxury into convenience.
