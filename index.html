About Us

We are an Agritech transforming the Informal Agri-value chain in Africa by enabling an efficient supply of fresh produce from small-scale farmers to informal Traders.

GreenKart is our B2B eCommerce platform enabling small-scale farmers to list crops that are recently planted; ready for harvest or available for sale to help farmers minimize Post-harvest losses.

  • Farmers close to another Share Transport to reduce up to 30% Logistics costs.
  • Traders can place an order for stock, and we deliver directly to their stall for free, within 24 hours.
  • We offer Buy-Now-Pay-Later to help Traders increase their buying during peak demands or in times of need.

How It Works

Crop Listing & Ordering

After sign-up & Location sharing, farmers can list crops that are ready for sale or harvest & Trader's place order via App or WhatsApp Chatbot

Aggregated Supply & Distributed Storage

Upon receiving an order, farmers close to each other can share transportation to reduce costs and deliver produce to our mobile cold storage containers

Delivery from Storage to Traders

Within 24 hours of placing an order, we deliver directly to Traders stalls for free using geo-locations

Services

We provide an e-commerce platform for both the informal trader and small-scale farmers who needs a market for their produce.

We are not a platform-only, we also operate decentralized mobile cold storage containers, allowing farmers to aggregate produce supply based on pre-orders, improving market access reducing post-harvest losses and lowering their logistics costs by up to 30%.
